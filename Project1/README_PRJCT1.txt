### Required Python Packages
*numpy 
*pandas
*matplotlib

   * Display a table of results and graphs.

## Expected Output
 **Terminal Output**: A table of values
 **Plots**:
  * Figure 1: Formula 1 (Truncation, Rounding, and Total Error)
  * Figure 2: Formula 2 (Truncation, Rounding, and Total Error)
